Our Story

Founded in 2010, Golden Crust Bakery began as a small family-owned shop with a passion for traditional baking methods. What started as a modest storefront with three employees has grown into a beloved local institution, all while maintaining our commitment to quality and craftsmanship.

We still use the same techniques and recipes passed down through generations, combining them with the finest locally sourced ingredients. Every item in our bakery is made from scratch daily - because we believe you can taste the difference that careful attention and time creates.

Meet our team of talented bakers who pour their heart into every creation, ensuring that each visit to Golden Crust is a memorable experience.
